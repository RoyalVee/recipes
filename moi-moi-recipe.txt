This is a recipe guide to making african moi moi

ingredient Needed
1) Beans
2) fresh pepper
3) oil
4) salt
5) maggi
6) cray fish
7) onoins
8) egg(optional)
9) fish(optional)


procedure:
step 1:
	pour your beans into a bow of water and wash.
step 2:
	pour your beans into a blender, add pepper, onoins, with little of warm water.
step 3:
	add your salt, maggi, oil and cray fish.
step 4:
	place mix in little bows and place in boiling water to cook.
